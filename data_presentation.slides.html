Office Indoor Air Quality Data Exploration¶

by Sandijs Vasilevskis¶

Investigation Overview¶

Indoor air quality parameters

Data from an office room in Slovenia

Two monitoring periods:

  1. BASELINE - first year - reference data (no interventions).
  2. MOBISTYLE - second year - employees are given a mobile app and sensors with LED lights (signaling a bad air quality in the office room) are installed.

Summary¶

IAQ parameters

  • Office temperature and window data has a strong correlation with outdoor air temperature
  • Office temperature and RH have distributions with two peaks (Seasonal data)
  • CO2 levels are stronlgy correlated with VOC levels, it means that both parameters are related to the indoor air polution from employees

Comfort categories

  • No clear evidence that the IAQ improved during the MOBISTYLE period
  • Overall IAQ comfort categories time distribution is slightly better for BASELINE
  • More time in Catgeory I and II both for Temperature and RH (COOLING)
  • CO2 and VOC level comfort categories are more equally distributed (HEATING, COOLING)

Window data

  • Employess had their window open more during the BASELINE period
  • And in general, more open during the COOLING seasons for both monitoring periods
